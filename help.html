Getting started

  • Install the extension: Load MoodleGPT 3 in developer mode or access the prebuilt CRX sent to you.
  • Add your Gemini key: Open the settings preview and drop your key once to unlock fallback answers.
  • Pick a quiz: Navigate to any Moodle quiz, press Alt+C, and watch local matches appear.

Automation toolkit

  • Delay window: Default 4-15 s pacing keeps attempts human. Raise it to 300 s for proctored runs.
  • Supported types: True/false and gap-select are solved automatically; multiple choice remains supervised.
  • Visibility toggles: Tap Alt+X to hide overlays if somebody walks by.

Troubleshooting

  • No matches found? Confirm your QA.json is loaded (321 entries ship with the extension).
  • Gemini fallback silent? Re-paste the API key and ensure context menu lookups are allowed.
  • Need a log? Generate a debug bundle from the extension help tab and email support.

Need more help?

Our support inbox responds every day. Send your debug bundle and the quiz link and we will reproduce the issue.
